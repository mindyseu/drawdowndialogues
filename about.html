Drawdown Dialogues About
Rutgers University × IDEO × Project Drawdown

Drawdown Dialogues is a conversation series between five climate leaders and 14 student designers. Drawdown is a critical turning point for life on earth. This is that moment in the future when greenhouse gases begin to decline. It indicates the reversion of climate change and global warming.

Project Drawdown is a solutions-based climate organization whose mission is to understand nature’s means of rebalancing the climate system. Design Practicum at Rutgers University, Mason Gross School of the Arts connects students with external organizations to create public-facing projects. This collaboration was facilitated by Joanne Cheung of IDEO and Professor Mindy Seu.
